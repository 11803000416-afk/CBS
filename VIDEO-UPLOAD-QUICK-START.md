## ❌ Troubleshooting Quick Fixes

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "File too large" | Video > 100MB. Compress it. Use HandBrake (free) |
| "Wrong format" | Use MP4 format, or convert first (CloudConvert) |
| "Won't upload" | Try another video, check internet, hard refresh browser |
| "Video not showing" | Refresh page, clear cache, wait 5+ seconds |
| "Drag-drop not working" | Use click to browse instead, update browser |

**Compress tool:** HandBrake (free, open-source)

## 📞 FAQ

**Q: Can I use videos from YouTube?**  
A: No. Must upload your own videos only.

**Q: How long will upload take?**  
A: Usually 5-30 seconds depending on file size.

**Q: Can I edit videos later?**  
A: Yes! Edit vehicle listing, add/remove videos anytime.

**Q: Will my videos be stored forever?**  
A: Yes, until you delete the vehicle or remove videos.

**Q: Can other sellers see my videos?**  
A: No, only viewers of your listing can see.

**Q: Can I download/export videos?**  
A: Only you can download (original file). Buyers cannot.

**Q: Maximum videos per car?**  
A: Technically unlimited, but recommend 3-5 for user experience.

**Q: Do videos slow down the website?**  
A: No. Videos are stored separately and loaded only when viewed.
